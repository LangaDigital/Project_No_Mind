About us

We believe in stories that spread ideas and capture attention.
We know that each brand has a unique narrative. We place that story at the centre of our work to connect with the right audience using digital technologies on mobile and the web.
We provide insight-driven digital marketing solutions that strengthen the narrative of brands and engage with consumers using technology.
Peter Langa

Founder & CEO

About Peter

I started off my career at The Creative Counsel where I worked on brands like Colgate Palmolive, Rickett Benkiser, Nestle and Vodacom. This was followed by a stint at Media24 where I enjoyed working for international magazines such as heat, FHM and Grazia.
I got bitten by the digital bug while doing my B.com Economics degree when I realised that the publishing industry was in trouble. I felt that new and interesting narratives had to be found to engage with consumers in a digital driven world. This thinking led me to do a digital course at Stellenbosch University and a few months later Langa Digital Agency was born.

Siya Africa

About Siya

Siya Africa is not from Africa, he is a global citizen who lives outside of the confines of time and space that restrain the way brands and organizations have been thinking about technology since he first learned MS-DOS as a child. He's a technologist who likes to experiment: whether it is using big data to find the best show to watch on Sunday night, or the Internet of Things to track his fitness goals, he lives a digital life that he wants to share with his clients and the world at large.
I completed my masters in Interactive Media Design at Indiana University, was named in the Mail & Guardian Young 200 2014 and serve as Executive Chair for Hacks/Hackers.
